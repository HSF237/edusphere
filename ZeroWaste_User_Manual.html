0Waste

Don't lose money to expired stock. The 2-minute quick start guide.

1 Logging a New Product

When a truck delivers new inventory, simply open the ZeroWaste app on your mobile browser.

  • Tap the large glowing Scan Line button at the bottom of your screen.
  • Point your camera at the barcode of the product.
  • If it's a new product, type the name. If you've scanned it before, it fills automatically!
  • Select the Expiry Date and hit Add to ZeroWaste. Done in 5 seconds.

2 The Dashboard Check

Your dashboard is your stock's health monitor.

  • Green items: Completely safe.
  • Orange items: Expiring soon. Move these items to the front row of your shelf so customers buy them first.
  • Red items: Expired. Remove from shelf immediately to avoid health risks.

3 WhatsApp Sentinels (PRO)

You don't even need to open the app to save money.

  • If an item is 7 days away from expiry, you will automatically receive a WhatsApp message at 8:00 AM.
  • Click the Flash Sale button in the message to automatically generate a promotional text you can send to your customers ("50% off milk expiring tomorrow!").
